Why Berlin?
Berlin is a city with exciting and important history.
The wall that divided the city for so long was finally taken down on November 9th, 1989.
“Now what belongs together will grow together.” - Willy Brandt, Berlin, November 10th 1989
This marked the new times for the city that now flurishes as one of the global hotspots for business, technology, and art.
